# Garmin Store Copy

Each text block below is intended to stay within Garmin's 4000-character field limit.

## English Description
Chars: 2087
```text
Track your EGYM circuit training directly from your wrist.

Support the project: https://buymeacoffee.com/forsterf

TRAINING MODES:

* Upper Body Circuit: 10 machines, classic EGYM strength circuit.
* Lower Body Circuit: 6 machines, focused leg training.
* Custom Circuit: Set machine order in Garmin Connect app settings.
* Individual Mode: Pick exercises freely and train at your own pace.

EGYM+ PROGRAMS:

Toggle EGYM+ membership in settings to access:

* ON: 32 smart plans across 8 program families (Adaptive, Negative, Explonic, Isokinetic).
* OFF: 4 basic programs (Endurance, Muscle Building, Toning, Strength).

FEATURES:

* Automatic target weight calculation based on personal RM values and program intensity.
* Learned weight calibration: adjusted weights are remembered for each exercise and training method.
* Quick Start: repeat your last saved setup directly from the start menu.
* Quality rating (%) or watt tracking for Explonic sets.
* Break timer with rest duration and the upcoming exercise.
* HR display with color-coded zones based on your Garmin profile.
* Strength test mode to establish or update RM values.
* FIT file recording with reps, weight, quality, watt, and workload per lap.
* Session summary with total volume (kg), calories, streak, records, and a simple trend vs the last workout.
* Data management: edit RM and watt values in Garmin Connect; changes sync to the watch.

NAVIGATE:

* UP/DOWN (or Swipe Up/Down): Adjust weight or quality.
* ENTER (or Tap): Confirm and advance.
* ESC: Go back or open the Save/Discard menu.
* Swipe Left: Skip exercise.
* Swipe Right: Undo the last step.

SUPPORTED MACHINES (20):

Chest Press, Ab Trainer, Seated Row, Oblique, Leg Press, Lat Pulldown, Butterfly, Back Extension, Reverse Butterfly, Shoulder Press, Squat, Leg Extension, Leg Curl, Abductor, Adductor, Hip Thrust, Bicep Curl, Tricep Press, Glute, Calf Raise.

GENERAL INFORMATION:

* Languages: English, German.
* Feedback: Send bug reports or feature requests via review or direct contact.
* Disclaimer: Not affiliated with or endorsed by EGYM GmbH.
```
